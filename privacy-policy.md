# Wingman Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Wingman is a Chrome extension designed to assist users with AI-powered email analysis, reminders, and productivity features inside Gmail.

## Information We Collect
Wingman may process email content temporarily in order to generate AI-powered insights and reminders. This processing occurs through secure backend services.

## Data Storage
User preferences and reminder settings may be stored locally using Chrome extension storage.

## Data Sharing
Wingman does not sell or share user data with third parties.

## Remote Processing
Some features require sending limited email content to a backend service for AI processing.
